## Retention: Stop the Leak

You can't fill a leaky bucket. Before spending $200K on acquisition, fix retention.

### Questions to Answer
- [ ] What's current churn rate? (% customers who don't return)
- [ ] Why do customers leave? (Price? Service quality? Moved?)
- [ ] What's the reactivation rate on lapsed customers?

### Retention Tactics ($30K budget)
| Tactic | Cost | Expected Impact |
