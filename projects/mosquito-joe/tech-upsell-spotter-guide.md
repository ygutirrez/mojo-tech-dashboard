## WHAT TO LOOK FOR (Upsell Triggers)

### üî∫ UPGRADE TO PREMIUM (Tick & Flea)
- Dogs or cats in the yard
- Wooded areas bordering the property
- Tall grass or leaf litter
- Customer mentions kids playing in grass
- Deer spotted or deer droppings
- Customer mentions finding ticks

**Say this:** "Hey, just so you know ‚Äî I noticed you've got some wooded area back there. Ticks love that. Might be worth asking about our Premium service that covers ticks too. Just something to think about."

---

### üî∫ UPGRADE TO BI-WEEKLY (From Monthly)
- Heavy shade / lots of trees
- Standing water (birdbaths, fountains, clogged gutters)
- Customer says "they're still pretty bad"
- Large property
- Near canals, lakes, retention ponds

**Say this:** "Your yard's got a lot of shade ‚Äî mosquitoes love that. If you're still seeing a lot between visits, bi-weekly might knock it out. The office can tell you more."

---

### üî∫ SPECIAL EVENT SPRAY
- Party decorations going up
- Tents or rental equipment on property
- Customer mentions upcoming event
- Graduation signs
- Wedding prep visible

**Say this:** "Oh, you've got something coming up? We do a special event spray ‚Äî extra strength, right before the party. Guarantees no mosquitoes for your guests. Want me to have the office call you about it?"

---

### üî∫ NATURAL / ORGANIC SERVICE
- Vegetable gardens
- Chicken coops
- Beehives
- "Organic" or eco-friendly signs/stickers
- Customer asks "what's in the spray?"
- Kids' play equipment everywhere

**Say this:** "I noticed your garden ‚Äî just so you know, we have an all-natural option if you're trying to keep things organic. Same protection, just plant-based. Office can give you details."

---

### üî∫ ADDITIONAL PROPERTY / REFERRALS

- Customer mentions family/friends with mosquito problems

**Say this:** "If you've got another property you want covered, we can set that up. Also, we've got a referral thing ‚Äî if your neighbor signs up, you both get a discount."

## HOW TO REPORT (After Every Job)

In your job notes or end-of-day report, include:

```
UPSELL SPOTTED:
- Customer: [Name / Address]
- What I saw: [Trigger - e.g., "dogs in yard, wooded border"]
- What I said: [Your seed comment]
- Suggested upsell: [Premium / Bi-weekly / Event / Natural]
- Customer reaction: [Interested / Maybe / Didn't engage]
```

**This gives CSRs ammo for the follow-up call.**
