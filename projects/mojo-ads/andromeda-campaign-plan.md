## Creative Brief: Video Production

### Video 1: "Backyard Transformation" (30 sec)
**Concept:** Split-screen before/after

**Shot List:**
- 0-5s: Family swatting mosquitoes, running inside at dusk
- 5-10s: MoJo truck arrives, tech spraying
- 10-20s: Same family relaxing outside, kids playing, no swatting
- 20-25s: "21 days of protection. Guaranteed."
- 25-30s: Offer + CTA

**Audio:** Upbeat, summer vibes. VO optional.

---

### Video 2: "POV Backyard Party" (15 sec - Reels)
**Concept:** Phone-style POV at a backyard BBQ

**Shot List:**
- 0-3s: POV walking into backyard party
- 3-8s: Pan across happy guests, no one swatting
- 8-12s: Text overlay: "The secret? We got sprayed yesterday."
- 12-15s: MoJo logo + "$49 first spray"

**Audio:** Trending audio or party ambience

---

### Video 3: "Tech Day in the Life" (45-60 sec)
**Concept:** Follow a MoJo tech for authenticity/trust

**Shot List:**
- Morning: Loading truck, checking equipment
- Arrival: Greeting homeowner
- Service: Spraying process (show thoroughness)
- Results: Customer testimonial snippet
- Close: "Book your spray - link in bio"

**Audio:** Interview style with tech, light music bed

---

### Video 4: "Misting System Reveal" (20 sec)
**Concept:** Showcase the premium product

**Shot List:**
- 0-5s: Beautiful Miami backyard, pool at sunset
- 5-10s: Close-up of discrete misting nozzle
- 10-15s: Mist activating (slow-mo, cinematic)
- 15-20s: "Automatic protection. Request a quote."

**Audio:** Cinematic/luxury feel, no VO needed

---

### Video 5: UGC-Style Testimonial (30 sec)
**Concept:** Real customer, phone-recorded feel

**Shot List:**
- Customer in their backyard talking to camera
- "We used to never use our backyard..."
- "Now the kids play outside every night"
- Show family enjoying yard
- "If you're on the fence, just try it"

**Audio:** Natural, no music, authenticity is key
