# The Back Nine - Franchisor Call Questions

**Purpose:** Validate the opportunity, understand unit economics, assess franchisor support  
**Context:** 4-partner group looking at 3+ territories in Miami area

---

## 1. Unit Economics & Financial Performance

**Critical â€” get real numbers, not averages**

- [ ] What's the **range** of performance across your franchise locations? (Top 25% vs bottom 25%)
- [ ] What separates high performers from low performers?
- [ ] What does **ramp-up** look like? Months 1-3, 3-6, 6-12?
- [ ] What's the typical **break-even timeline**?
- [ ] Can you share **actual P&Ls** from existing franchisees (anonymized)?
- [ ] What are the **biggest operating expenses** we should budget for?
- [ ] What's the **member churn rate** you're seeing across locations?
- [ ] What's the average **revenue per member** (including non-membership spending)?

---

## 2. The "No Employees" Model

**This is a big selling point â€” validate it**

- [ ] How does day-to-day operations work with no staff?
- [ ] What technology/systems enable the self-service model?
- [ ] What's the **actual time commitment** from owners? Hours per week?
- [ ] When do owners typically need to hire? At what revenue level?
- [ ] Who handles **equipment maintenance** and troubleshooting?
- [ ] How do you handle **security** with 24/7 unmanned access?
- [ ] What happens when something breaks at 2am and a member is there?

---

## 3. Territory & Multi-Unit Strategy

**Critical for our 4-partner, 3-territory approach**

- [ ] What territories are available in **South Florida / Miami-Dade**?
- [ ] How do you define territory boundaries? (Population, geography, demographics?)
- [ ] What's your **Area Development Agreement** structure for multi-unit buyers?
- [ ] Can we **lock in pricing** for future territories beyond the initial 3?
- [ ] What's the **required opening timeline** for multi-territory deals?
- [ ] Do you offer **development incentives** for groups committing to 3+ units?
- [ ] How many multi-unit franchisees do you have currently?

---

## 4. Site Selection & Build-Out

- [ ] What's the **ideal square footage** range?
- [ ] What type of locations work best? (Retail, industrial, mixed-use?)
- [ ] What's the **typical build-out cost**? Range we should expect?
- [ ] How long does build-out typically take?
- [ ] Do you have preferred vendors or can we source locally?
- [ ] What **equipment** is included vs. additional cost?
- [ ] What simulators do you use and what's the replacement cycle?

---

## 5. Franchisee Support & Training

- [ ] Walk me through the **training program** â€” length, location, content?
- [ ] What **ongoing support** do you provide after opening?
- [ ] How often do you communicate with franchisees?
- [ ] Do you have a **franchisee advisory council**?
- [ ] What marketing support do you provide? National campaigns?
- [ ] How do you help with **local marketing** and member acquisition?
- [ ] What technology/software is provided? CRM? POS? Scheduling?

---

## 6. Franchise System Health

**They're new â€” need to understand stability**

- [ ] How many **total locations** are open today? (Corporate + franchise)
- [ ] How many are **in development**?
- [ ] Have any franchisees **closed or failed**? What happened?
- [ ] Have any franchisees **resold** their territories? At what valuation?
- [ ] What's your **franchisee satisfaction** score? Do you survey?
- [ ] What's your **growth plan** for the next 2-3 years?
- [ ] How is the franchisor **funded**? Any outside investors?

---

## 7. Validation & References

- [ ] Can you connect us with **3-5 existing franchisees** for validation calls?
- [ ] Specifically, any in **Florida** or similar markets?
- [ ] Any franchisees who started with **multiple territories**?
- [ ] Can we visit an existing location in person?

---

## 8. Legal & FDD

- [ ] When can we receive the **FDD**?
- [ ] Have there been any **litigation** or disputes with franchisees?
- [ ] What are the **renewal terms** at end of initial agreement?
- [ ] What are the **transfer/resale restrictions**?
- [ ] What happens if a partner wants to exit?

---

## 9. Miami-Specific Questions

- [ ] Do you have any locations in **Florida** currently?
- [ ] How do you see the **Miami market** for indoor golf?
- [ ] Any concerns about **seasonality** in a warm-weather market?
- [ ] Who would be our **regional support** contact?

---

## 10. Partnership Structure

**Important for our 4-partner situation**

- [ ] How do you handle **multi-owner franchise agreements**?
- [ ] Do all partners need to go through training?
- [ ] Who needs to be the **designated operator**?
- [ ] Any restrictions on **passive investors** in the ownership group?
