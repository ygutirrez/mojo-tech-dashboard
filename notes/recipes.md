## Carnivore / Low-Carb

### Parmesan Cheese Balls
**Source:** [Instagram Reel](https://www.instagram.com/reel/DUBnWMrjvKW/)

Crispy baked parmesan bites — carnivore-friendly snack.

**Typical method:**
1. Shred fresh parmesan (or use pre-shredded)
2. Form small mounds on parchment-lined baking sheet
3. Bake at 400°F for 5-7 min until golden and crispy
4. Let cool (they crisp up more as they cool)

**Variations:**
- Add a pinch of everything bagel seasoning before baking
- Shape around a small piece of pepperoni or bacon
